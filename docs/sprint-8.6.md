# Sprint 8.6 – 247SP Production Readiness

## Objective

Transform 24/7 Sales Partner from a functional prototype into a production-ready product capable of supporting the first paying customer.

No new Ultimate Back Office modules will be developed during Sprint 8.6. Development effort is focused exclusively on completing the operational workflows required to sell, provision, support, and operate 24/7 Sales Partner.

---

# Goals

Complete every workflow required for a customer to:

* Purchase 24/7 Sales Partner
* Complete onboarding
* Receive a working website
* Capture leads
* Manage those leads
* Maintain billing
* Operate without manual database changes

---

# Milestone 1 — Lead Capture Pipeline

## Objective

Every lead generated from a customer website must automatically enter Lead Hub.

### Deliverables

* Website contact forms submit successfully.
* Contact form validation.
* Spam protection.
* Lead creation.
* Lead assignment.
* Lead activity history.
* Email notification to customer.
* Internal notification.

### Validation

* Submit form.
* Lead appears in Lead Hub.
* Customer receives notification.
* Activity logged.

# Deferred Until After Launch

The following remain intentionally out of scope until 24/7 Sales Partner is production-ready:

* EMD Network
* Super Simple Payments
* Tell Us How We Did
* Additional Ultimate Back Office modules
* Appointment scheduling
* Instant quote calculators
* AI-generated content
* Advanced SEO automation
* Service area page expansion
* Additional service/SEO bundles
* Customer self-service service-page management

---

# Exit Criteria

Sprint 8.6 is complete when a brand-new customer can purchase 24/7 Sales Partner, complete onboarding, receive a functioning website, generate leads, manage those leads, and maintain their subscription without requiring manual intervention from the development team.
