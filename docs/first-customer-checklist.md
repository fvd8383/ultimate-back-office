# First Paying Customer Definition

A customer can:

1. Sign up
2. Create a business
3. Activate 247SP
4. Pay setup fee
5. Pay monthly subscription
6. Choose domain
7. Receive website
8. Receive business email
9. Access Lead Hub
10. Manage billing

Without manual database changes.
